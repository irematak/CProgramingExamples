## FIND TRANSPOSE OF A MATRIX   

The transpose of a matrix is a new matrix that is obtained by exchanging the rows and columns.

* **Example** 

> **Input**  
> Enter size of square matrix: 3   
> Enter elements of the matrix...   
  1 3 9 4 2 7 6 5 8     
> **Output**   
> 1  3  9    
  4  2  7   
  6  5  8   
> Transpose of the matrix:  
> 1  4  6     
  3  2  5  
  9  7  8
